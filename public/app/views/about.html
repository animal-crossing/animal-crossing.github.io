Hi there villager,

in this site you can save your collection of Animal Crossing Amiibo Cards. This site was developed by @AlgusDark, if you have a problem with the site, don't hesitate to contact him.

Sections

+ All

List of all the Animal Crossing Amiibo Cards (of the corresponding serie)

+ Collection

Wanna know how many cards do you have? this is the section where you can see that.

+ Favorites

Every person has a favorite neighbor, here you can see all your favorite cards.

+ Missing

One of the best features in this page. Share this section to friends so they can see your missing cards, also they can see what are the favorites cards you don't have, maybe that's the first cards you want in your collection.

+ Repeated

It's a shame that you have repeated cards, here you can see those and also share to others, so they can easily request a trade

+ Visit village

Search for a specific friend or look through other villagers and their collections.
